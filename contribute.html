How Can You Contribute?

We welcome contributions from individuals who share our vision. Whether you are a developer, designer, or passionate about internet freedom, there are various ways you can contribute.

Here are some ways you can contribute:

  • Developers can contribute by creating tools and resources that help people circumvent censorship and surveillance.
  • Designers can contribute by creating designs for tools and resources.
  • Anyone can contribute by spreading the word about the project.

If you are interested in contributing, please contact us at 8080@uninhibited.sbs
