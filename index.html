What is this?

The 8080 Project is a project to host and develop a collection of tools and resources to help people circumvent censorship and surveillance.

Our Mission

At The 8080 Project, we believe in the free flow of information. Our mission is to provide tools and resources that empower individuals to access and share information without restrictions.

How Can You Contribute?

We welcome contributions from individuals who share our vision. Whether you are a developer, designer, or passionate about internet freedom, there are various ways you can contribute. Visit our Contribute page to learn more.
